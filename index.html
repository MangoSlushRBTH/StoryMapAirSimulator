Air quality assessment

CO₂: human emission ~200 ml/min resting, scales with activity. VOCs: human skin/breath ~500 µg/hr resting; biological output (H₂S, methane, indole, skatole) adds 50–520 µg/hr. PM2.5: paraffin candle 10–100 mg/hr (35 mg/hr average used); beeswax ~6 mg/hr; soy ~8 mg/hr; scented paraffin ~55 mg/hr. Soft furnishings increase PM surface deposition rate by 30–80%.
