Air quality assessment

VOC perceptual index = Σ(compound concentration / odour detection threshold). Thresholds used: H₂S 0.7 µg/m³; indole 0.04 µg/m³; skatole 0.006 µg/m³; acrolein 140 µg/m³; acetone 47,600 µg/m³; isoprene 27,000 µg/m³; toluene 1,100 µg/m³; formaldehyde 60 µg/m³; benzene 4,800 µg/m³. Index 1.0 = detectable; 3.0 = clearly unpleasant; 5.0 = headache/nausea threshold (sensitive individuals); 10+ = intolerable. PM2.5 WHO 24-hr guideline: 15 µg/m³. Sources: Nagata (2003) odour thresholds; Wallace et al. (2004); Petry et al. (2014); Weschler (2011); ASHRAE 62.1.
